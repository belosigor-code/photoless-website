## COMPETITOR_BRIEF

### 1. Grether Photography — grether-photography.com
- Serves Zurich/Aargau including Regensdorf
- Offers: events, business, real estate, drone, portrait, architecture, family
- Strengths: clear service taxonomy, recognizable client logos, geographic targeting
- Weaknesses: no pricing, no testimonials despite listing clients, outdated design, scattered CTAs
- Primary CTA: "Termin vereinbaren"

### 2. Photoart (Fabian Henzmann) — photoart.ch
- Specializes in professional headshots and corporate portraits
- Strengths: strong testimonials from Swiss firms, clear niche, direct online scheduling
- Weaknesses: dense homepage, German-only, no pricing visible
- Primary CTA: "Online Termin" via Calendly

### 3. Thomas Hinder Fotografie — thomashinder.com
- Headshots, weddings, corporate, fashion, video; based in Zürich Oerlikon
- Strengths: transparent pricing (CHF 89 application photos), 15+ years experience, bilingual
- Weaknesses: complex nav, multiple conflicting CTAs, dated design
- Primary CTA: "Online Booking" for discounted session

### 4. Select Fotostudio — select-fotostudio.ch
- Application photos from CHF 49, business portraits; near Zurich HB
- Strengths: professional branding, multilingual, prime location
- Weaknesses: extremely weak content, almost no portfolio, unclear CTA, relies on Facebook
- Primary CTA: Facebook social media (!)

### 5. Maren Kindler Photography — marenkindler.com
- Headshots and personal branding; 173+ reviews, 5-star rated
- Strengths: outstanding social proof, clear methodology, international awards
- Weaknesses: German-only, heavy scripts, no pricing, premium-only positioning
- Primary CTA: "Fotoshooting buchen"

### Competitor Gaps We Can Own
- None of the competitors combine photography + wellness under one roof
- Most have weak or absent testimonials
- Pricing transparency is rare — only Thomas Hinder shows prices
- Most design is generic/template-looking — strong visual identity is a differentiator
- Online booking is only available at 2 of 5 competitors
