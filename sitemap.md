# Sitemap — Photoless

## Pages (all in main navigation)
- index.html        — Home: portfolio tease, services overview, CTA to book
- portrait.html     — Portrait: individuals, teams, corporate headshots
- reportage.html    — Reportage: events, corporate days, documentary
- werbung.html      — Werbefotografie: product, brand, advertising photography
- dokumente.html    — Dokumentarfotos: passport, ID, visa, CV photos
- hochzeit.html     — Hochzeitsfotografie: weddings around Regensdorf/Zurich
- kontakt.html      — Kontakt: booking form, address, phone, hours

## User Flows

### Flow 1: Business client
1. Google "Werbefotograf Zürich" → Homepage
2. Service cards → Werbefotografie page
3. "Projekt besprechen" CTA → Kontakt form → conversion

### Flow 2: Wedding couple
1. Google "Hochzeitsfotograf Regensdorf" → Homepage or Hochzeit page
2. Gallery + testimonials → trust
3. "Datum anfragen" → Kontakt form → conversion

### Flow 3: Document photo walk-in
1. Google "Passfoto Regensdorf" → Dokumentarfotos page
2. Pricing + hours → walk in or book

## Rationale
- Wellness removed: keeps brand focused on premium photography positioning
- Dokumentarfotos as standalone page: drives local search traffic, distinct funnel
- Kontakt as full page: primary goal is bookings — form deserves dedicated space
- No blog: no content to populate, dilutes premium feel
