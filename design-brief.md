# Design Brief — Photoless
## Direction: Warme Textur

### Palette
```
--color-bg:      #f6f2e9   warm cream — paper-like, organic
--color-surface: #ede9de   warm linen for cards/sections
--color-primary: #1b1f18   near-black with subtle green undertone
--color-accent:  #7c9e79   muted sage green — spring meadow, fresh not loud
--color-text:    #2d3129   dark warm olive-brown body text
```

### Typography
- Heading: Lora (Google Fonts) — contemporary literary serif, brushstroke origins, warm but authoritative
- Body: Source Sans 3 (Google Fonts) — humanist, legible, friendly without being casual

### Mood
Human · Warm · Quietly luxurious
Not cold, not clinical. Feels like a beautifully designed editorial magazine or a premium artisan brand.
Appeals equally to wedding clients (romantic, warm) and business clients (approachable, trustworthy).

### Layout Feel
Organic section shapes with slight asymmetry. Full-bleed warm cream sections with terracotta accent blocks.
Typography-led with Lora at large scale. Photography floats in rounded-corner frames.
Sections transition with colour — tonal shifts rather than hard dividers.
